    • Claws vs Claws

    Open 18th July - 1st August 2023

    art by Limbow

    Claws vs Claws is a 2-week music competition hosted on the Claws Discord server. After voting on a central theme, participants compete for their song to win in one of the following categories:

  • — Best Overall

    • Our pick for the best submission. (50% audience vote)

  • — Best Theme Interpretation

    • The song that best embraces the theme we choose.

  • — Best Experimental

    • The song that best balances sound quality and creativity.

  • — Best Vocal

    • This category is to encourage people to work with vocalists.

  • — Best Acoustic Focus

    • The best track that fully embraces acoustic instruments. (Synths can be used but not the main sound)

    We set these categories to encourage people to escape their comfort zones and create something new and unique. To encourage people to collaborate in groups, we allow participants to be part of up to 3 submissions. However, solo tracks are still acceptable.

    Following the event, the best songs will be released as an album: firstly debuting on SoundCloud and later releasing on major streaming platforms. (If your song isn't selected, you are free to release it by yourself)

    We hope you'll get involved and spread the word about this event. All past events have been an opportunity for making new friendships and forging fond memories. Together, we can create something incredible.

    Good luck!
